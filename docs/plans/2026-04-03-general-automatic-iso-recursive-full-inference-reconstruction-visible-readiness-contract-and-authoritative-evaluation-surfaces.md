## Reconstruction-Visible Readiness Contract

Positive `P6` for the current full-inference family requires one exact
current-architecture story to remain visible from item-3 admission through the
authoritative output surfaces.

The exact positive bar is:

1. The same item-3-admitted candidate family and route must remain
   identifiable all the way through the read. For the current family, that
   means the evidence must stay inside the named item-3 route arms
   `rootNonLocalSchemeAliasBaseLike` or
   `sameLaneLocalRetainedChildTarget`, with the retained-child read still
   respecting `boundHasForallFrom`, `keepTargetFinal`, and `targetC` where
   applicable.
2. `TyMu`-side recursive shape must survive as reviewable `TMu` output for the
   same recursive story. Hidden unfolding, route swapping, or a new helper
   interpretation does not count as continuity.
3. `ERoll` / `EUnroll` continuity must remain consistent with that same
   recursive story rather than appearing as a disconnected elaboration-side
   artifact.
4. `runPipelineElab`, `runPipelineElabChecked`, and the matching public facade
   in `src-public/MLF/Pipeline.hs` must expose the recursive structure without
   disagreement or rescue. The matching internal facade in
   `src/MLF/Elab/Pipeline.hs` must remain continuous with those same
   authoritative entrypoints rather than providing a second interface.
5. Reviewers must be able to point to the same recursive story directly on the
   authoritative current surfaces. Packet-local folklore, replay-only
   reinterpretation, or helper-side archaeology does not count.

Under that bar, item `4` freezes the March 25 lawful outcome vocabulary as
the only lawful classification vocabulary for later rounds:

| Outcome | Item-4 meaning on the current authoritative surfaces |
| --- | --- |
| `stable visible persistence` | the same admitted family, owner / binder story, route meaning, `TyMu` to `TMu` continuity, and `ERoll` / `EUnroll` story remain intact and review-visible on `runPipelineElab`, `runPipelineElabChecked`, and the matching public facade |
| `admitted but not reconstruction-visible / blocker debt` | item-3 admission or some downstream continuity exists, but one or more authoritative surfaces lose, hide, or disagree about the recursive structure, so the result is not yet positive `P6` |
| `fail-closed rejection` | the candidate drifts, becomes ambiguous, crosses a forbidden quantified boundary, changes owner / binder / route meaning, or survives only through manual reinterpretation, fallback-like rescue, or a widened interface / search story |

Only `stable visible persistence` may count as positive `P6` evidence later.

The following are explicitly insufficient even if `TyMu`, `TMu`, `ERoll`, or
`EUnroll` appears somewhere in the record:

- solver-only success;
- helper-only success;
- witness-only success;
- fallback-only success;
- internal-only success; and
- packet-history-only success.

Those cases may at most count as
`admitted but not reconstruction-visible / blocker debt`, or they must remain
`fail-closed rejection`. They do not earn a readiness upgrade.

## Evidence Sufficiency And Insufficiency

This artifact does not award repo-level readiness. It freezes what later
rounds would need to show before any repo-level readiness claim could be
honest.

Evidence later may count as sufficient for a repo-level readiness claim only
if all of the following are true:

- representative reruns cover the named positive slices for `P2`, `P3`,
  `P4`, `P5`, and `P6`, plus the still-live negative / pressure slices
  carrying `N1`, `N2`, and `N6`;
- those reruns succeed or fail closed on the authoritative current surfaces
  `runPipelineElab`, `runPipelineElabChecked`, and the matching internal /
  public pipeline facades, rather than only on supporting seams;
- the observed continuity matches the current-architecture item-3 route
  families and guards instead of introducing a new route, a second interface,
  or a widened search story;
- the recursive story stays review-visible as `TyMu` to `TMu` continuity plus
  consistent `ERoll` / `EUnroll` continuity without disagreement or rescue;
  and
- the aggregate evidence is representative enough to support a family-wide
  read rather than a one-packet exception.

The following remain insufficient for any repo-level readiness claim:

- one packet only;
- solver admission only;
- fallback output only;
- helper-visible continuity only;
- historical predecessor truth only;
- any result that would need ambiguity ranking under `N1`;
- any result that would need fallback widening;
- any result that would need cyclic search or multi-SCC search; and
- any result that would need a second interface or other boundary revision.

If later evidence can be explained only by one packet, by non-authoritative
continuity, or by a widened interface / search story, item `4` requires the
read to stay below repo-level readiness. At most it may remain
`admitted but not reconstruction-visible / blocker debt`; otherwise it must
remain `fail-closed rejection`.
